# Amazon Brand Launch Checklist

A step-by-step operational guide for launching new products on 
Amazon — from brand registry to first sale.

## Launch Phases

### Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-2)
- Brand Registry application
- Competitor analysis & pricing strategy
- Keyword research & listing creation
- Product photography requirements
- A+ Content design brief

### Phase 2: Launch (Weeks 3-4)
- Listing upload & indexing verification
- PPC campaign creation (launch architecture)
- Initial pricing strategy
- Inventory forecasting setup

### Phase 3: Optimization (Weeks 5-8)
- PPC bid optimization
- Listing A/B testing
- Review generation strategy
- Keyword ranking tracking

### Phase 4: Scale (Months 3+)
- International marketplace expansion
- Campaign architecture expansion
- Variation strategy
- Seasonal planning
